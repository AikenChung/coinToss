Tossing a Coin

1. Select the side of the coin you want to bet.

2. Type In your name and the amount of ether you want to put in.

3. Click "Make A Bet"

4. Waiting for your result displayed in the "Your Betting Result" section below.

5. If you win, you will have a double amount of the money you bet.

6. If you lose, you cannot have your money back.

7. You can make a bet again only if you got previous bet result back.
